# Dream Story

Driving. MC staring turns the radio up. Rain begins to pick up along with wiper blades.

MC focusing on the lines of the road on the winding drive.

Time passes

Another driver along an empty part of the road passes the MC car doing at least double the speed of his. MC doesnt realize it yet but that will be the last vehicle they will see in some time.

Eventually MC care sputteres and stalls forcing them to pull over alongside the road. Waiting and trying to contact people. Introducing us to some of the people in the MC life. 

After failing to get in touch with anyone they get out, putting their feet on the ground and stepping out. From awkward hunched to crouched and stretching slowing stretching their legs and looking along the road ahead they hear some cracking in the woods off to their right on the edge of the tree line. 

### Synopsis

The story tells of a char who is going back and forth with 2 lives. One of the waking world the other of a world of darkness and dreams.

- Zorya  Description: Slavic goddess of twilight or dawn, linked to the night sky’s transitions.  Etymology: Slavic zora (dawn, aurora).
- Rhea  Description: A moon of Saturn and a Titaness, symbolizing maternal strength and lunar cycles. Etymology: Greek, possibly rheo (to flow).
- Hypnos  Description: Greek god of sleep, tied to dreams and the subconscious of night.  Etymology: Greek hypnos (sleep).

# Offering

A tech thriller of murder and internet rumors lead a woman to track down the truth of what happened to her father after a seemingly unrelated web of characters begin to hint at a larger truth for why her father left their family lost his mind and killed 20 people at his job only to be found dead 15 miles out in the woods.

The story leads a woman in search of clues and paranoia into a web of net-slooths who she believes led him to all of sudden act like a crazy person. As she tracks her fathers decent into madness she begins to find out that the cult like antifa members were just a convient scape goat for a larger cult like cabal.

With all of the answers she finds things dont quiet add up even if they had given her father proper motive and resources. As she gets closer to finding out what happened to her family (after having been torn apart in highschool by her father leaving suddenly) she begins to see the message underneath of the clues only by being the closest person in her fathers life does she have the unique perspective to understand what was actually possible of a good one family man.
